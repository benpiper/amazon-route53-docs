.cz (Czech Republic)

Return to index

Registration and renewal period
One to ten years.

Restrictions
Open to the public, with no restrictions.

Privacy protection
Not supported, but email address and phone number are hidden for all contacts.

Domain locking to prevent unauthorized transfers
Supported.

Internationalized domain names
Not supported.

Authorization code required for transfer to Route 53
Yes
If your current registrar doesn’t provide an authorization code, go to https://www.nic.cz/whois/send-password/ to request it to be sent to the registrant email address by the CZ domain registry.

DNSSEC
Not supported.

Deadlines for renewing and restoring domains
+ Renewal is possible: Until the expiration date + Late renewal with Route 53 is possible: Until 58 days after expiration + Domain is deleted from Route 53: 59 days after expiration + Restoration with the registry is possible: No + Domain is deleted from the registry: 60 days after expiration
