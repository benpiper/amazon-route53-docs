Registration and renewal period
One to five years.

Restrictions
The registry for .co domains, Go.co, considers some domain names to be premium domain names. You can’t register premium .co domains with or transfer them to Route 53. For more information, see the Go.co website.

Privacy protection (applies to all contact types: person, company, association, and public body)
All information is hidden.

Domain locking to prevent unauthorized transfers
Supported.

Internationalized domain names
Supported.

Authorization code required for transfer to Route 53
Yes

DNSSEC
Not supported.

Deadlines for renewing and restoring domains
+ Renewal is possible: Until the expiration date + Late renewal with Route 53 is possible: Until 29 days after expiration + Domain is deleted from Route 53: 30 days after expiration + Restoration with the registry is possible: Between 30 days and 45 days after expiration + Domain is deleted from the registry: 50 days after expiration
